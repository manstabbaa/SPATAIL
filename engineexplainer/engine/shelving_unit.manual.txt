FLATWOOD 3-TIER SHELVING UNIT  ·  Model FW-380  ·  80 × 30 × 90 cm

KNOW YOUR PARTS
This unit has seven flat panels: a BASE, a TOP, two SIDE PANELS (left and
right), a BACK PANEL, and two SHELVES. All panels connect with cam-lock
fasteners — no glue required.

STEP 1 - LAY OUT THE BASE
Place the BASE flat on the floor with the pre-drilled holes facing up.

STEP 2 - STAND THE SIDE PANELS
Stand the LEFT SIDE PANEL and the RIGHT SIDE PANEL upright on the ends of the
base. Line up each side panel's bottom holes with the base and lock them.

STEP 3 - FIT THE TOP PANEL
Lower the TOP PANEL onto the two side panels so it caps the frame. Lock the
top into both side panels.

STEP 4 - SLIDE IN THE BACK PANEL
Slide the BACK PANEL into the rear groove of the frame. It squares the unit
and keeps the shelves from sliding backward.

STEP 5 - INSTALL THE LOWER SHELF
Rest the LOWER SHELF on the lower set of supports between the side panels and
press it down until it seats.

STEP 6 - INSTALL THE UPPER SHELF
Rest the UPPER SHELF on the upper set of supports the same way.

STEP 7 - CHECK THE UNIT
Make sure every cam-lock is tight and the unit stands square before loading it.
