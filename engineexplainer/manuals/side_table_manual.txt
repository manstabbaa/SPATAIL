NORDLI Side Table — Assembly Instructions

Thank you for purchasing the NORDLI side table. Please read these instructions
fully before you begin. Assembly takes about 10 minutes and requires only the
included Allen key.

PARTS LIST
- 1 x Tabletop (square, 55 cm x 55 cm, 5 cm thick)
- 4 x Table leg (9 cm x 9 cm square section, 45 cm long)
- 8 x Wood screw (M6 x 40 mm)
- 1 x Allen key

Step 1 — Lay out the parts.
Find a clean, flat surface. Place the tabletop face-down on a soft cloth so you
do not scratch the finish. Confirm you have all four legs and eight screws.

Step 2 — Attach the first leg.
Stand a leg upright in the front-left corner of the underside of the tabletop.
Drive two screws through the leg bracket into the tabletop and tighten them with
the Allen key. Do not overtighten.

Step 3 — Attach the remaining three legs.
Repeat for the front-right, back-left, and back-right corners. Tighten all eight
screws evenly so every leg sits square to the top.

Step 4 — Stand the table upright.
Turn the table the right way up and set it on the floor. Press gently on each
corner to confirm it does not wobble. If it rocks, check that all four legs are
fully tightened.

Caution: do not stand on the table. Maximum load 15 kg.
